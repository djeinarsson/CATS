Cat Skills.

They entertain us to make us laugh.

We are privileged to share our homes with cats and they are so good at constantly reminding us of that fact. How so? They jump into our laps to warm us on cold winter nights. They entertain us to make us laugh and smile when we're down. They are chicken soup for our bodies, minds, and spirits.

Meow meow meow...

A cat’s meow isn’t just for display. It’s your cat’s way of having a conversation with you; telling you about their day, asking you what’s for dinner, when the next cuddle time is. Cats only meow at humans; this vocal communication is reserved especially for you. Not only does this ‘cat language’ help you bond with your cat, but it also means you always have a paw-some buddy to chat to.
